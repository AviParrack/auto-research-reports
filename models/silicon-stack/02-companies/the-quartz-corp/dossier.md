## Overview

The Quartz Corp holds a near-monopoly on the world's **highest-purity quartz**,
mined at Spruce Pine, NC — the ultimate source of crucible- and optics-grade
silica feeding the chip stack. *(Scaffold stub.)*

## Position in the chain

- **Supplies:** [high-purity quartz](/models/silicon-stack/inputs/) used to make crucibles
  for crystal growth (and, indirectly, the silicon itself).
- **Downstream:** smelters like [Ferroglobe](/models/silicon-stack/companies/ferroglobe/) and
  wafer makers like [Shin-Etsu](/models/silicon-stack/companies/shin-etsu/).
