## Overview

Ferroglobe is a representative producer of **metallurgical-grade silicon** — the
bulk commodity silicon that feeds polysilicon purification. *(Scaffold stub.)*

## Position in the chain

- **Consumes:** [high-purity quartz](/models/silicon-stack/inputs/) and carbon.
- **Supplies:** MG-Si to polysilicon makers like [Wacker](/models/silicon-stack/companies/wacker/).
- Carbothermic reduction is energy-intensive — see the [Energetic Index](/models/silicon-stack/energetic-index/).
