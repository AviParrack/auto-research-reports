## Overview

Wacker is a leading producer of **semiconductor-grade polysilicon** (eleven-nines
purity) — the feedstock for single-crystal ingots. *(Scaffold stub.)*

## Position in the chain

- **Consumes:** metallurgical-grade silicon from smelters like [Ferroglobe](/models/silicon-stack/companies/ferroglobe/).
- **Supplies:** polysilicon to wafer makers like [Shin-Etsu](/models/silicon-stack/companies/shin-etsu/) — see [Inputs](/models/silicon-stack/inputs/).
- The Siemens process is energy-intensive — see the [Energetic Index](/models/silicon-stack/energetic-index/).
