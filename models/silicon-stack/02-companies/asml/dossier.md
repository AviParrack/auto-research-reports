## Overview

ASML is the **sole supplier** of extreme-ultraviolet (EUV) lithography — the
single hardest chokepoint in the leading-edge stack. *(Scaffold stub.)*

## Internal process highlights

- EUV systems integrate ~100,000 parts, Zeiss precision optics, and a tin-plasma
  13.5 nm light source.
- A limited number of systems ship per year at very high price; amortized per
  wafer this is a major cost driver — see [Inputs](/models/silicon-stack/inputs/).

## Position in the chain

- **Supplies:** EUV tools to leading-edge fabs like [TSMC](/models/silicon-stack/companies/tsmc/).
- **Upstream:** Zeiss optics, Cymer/EUV light source (not yet modelled).
- See the [Energetic Index](/models/silicon-stack/energetic-index/) for tool energy intensity.
