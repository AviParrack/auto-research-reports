# Helios Project — Molten Regolith Electrolysis Demonstration

## technology

"The Helios Project has developed an electrochemical reactor capable of
extracting oxygen from lunar regolith, which is made of approximately 45%
oxygen, using a process called Molten Regolith Electrolysis — melting the
lunar regolith and electrolyzing it to separate oxygen from various metals."

## partners

- ispace (Japan) — Lunar Surface Access Service (LSAS) mission carrier
- Israel Space Agency — support
- Eta Space — cryogenic O2 liquefaction and storage partnership (2022)
