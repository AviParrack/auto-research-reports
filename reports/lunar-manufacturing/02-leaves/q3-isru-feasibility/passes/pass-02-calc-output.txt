--- Acceleration-regime TRL decomposition (anti-pattern #11) ---

  Under TAI-grade automation compression (TAI-C):
    * Demonstration cadence rises ~10×: each MRE/carbothermal lab
      iteration that took 18 months drops to ~6-10 weeks
    * Earth-feedback loop tightens via robotic CLPS demos
      → 2-3 CLPS demos per year vs 1 every 2 years today
    * Capital-mass compression in plant designs (q4 lever)
    * Result: carbothermal + MRE reach TRL 8 (flight-demo'd, returning
      product) by 2028-2030; structural-mass loops by 2032-2035.

  Under business-as-usual (BAU):
    * NASA budget remains constrained; Artemis schedule slips ~1y/yr
    * CLPS missions deliver O2 ISRU demos but no product flow
    * Carbothermal reaches TRL 7 by 2030, TRL 8 by 2035-2040
    * MRE reaches TRL 6-7 by 2030, TRL 8 by 2035-2040

  Under stall (50-year-Apollo-drought analog):
    * Political will collapses; CLPS programme winds down
    * Carbothermal stays at TRL 6 indefinitely (Sierra demo
      becomes a museum piece)
    * MRE remains TRL 4 (Sadoway lab + Helios sim chamber)
    * No lunar product flow at any horizon

  The TRL trajectory depends on regime, not calendar year. This
  decomposition is the q3 deliverable for q4/q8 synthesis: q4's
  cost numerator is meaningless if regime = stall, because no
  product exists to ship; q4's framework is binding only under
  BAU or TAI-C.
