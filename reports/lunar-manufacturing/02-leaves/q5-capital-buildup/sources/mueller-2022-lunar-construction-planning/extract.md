## Abstract

Mueller is one of NASA-KSC's senior lunar-construction architects (co-author of Metzger et al. 2013 bootstrap). This NTRS 2022 report lays out a phased buildup of lunar surface construction: pre-precursor robotic site preparation, landing-pad construction, ISRU plant emplacement, habitat module landing, mobility-asset deployment, and progressive infrastructure densification. The paper presents construction-method trades (sintering, sulfur-concrete, geopolymers, fused-regolith bricks) and equipment-design trades (excavators, regolith-handling rovers, additive-manufacturing systems).
