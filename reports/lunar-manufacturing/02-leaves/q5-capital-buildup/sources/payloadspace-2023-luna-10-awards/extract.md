## Abstract

Payload coverage of the DARPA LunA-10 contract announcements, with company-by-company focus area assignments where disclosed: ICON (3D printing structures using lunar regolith), Sierra Space (oxygen extraction from regolith), Firefly Aerospace (on-orbit spacecraft hub framework), GITAI (modular Inchworm robots for construction/maintenance), Redwire (communications and PNT services). Confirms seven-month study scope, public presentations in April 2024, and final reports due July 2024.
