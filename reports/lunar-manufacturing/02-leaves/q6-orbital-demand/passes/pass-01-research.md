## What's covered

- **All five use cases:** space-based data centers (SDC), space-based solar power (SBSP), orbital propellant depots, Artemis lunar surface cargo, Mars architecture mass, satellite servicing.
- **The full viability range for orbital DC:** Handmer's 2× cost premium (mid-optimist), McCalip's 3.2× cost multiple (skeptic), Cote's bandwidth-binding-constraint (structural skeptic), Metzger's exponential-AI-conditional (conditional optimist).
- **Per-MW orbital DC mass:** peraspera 30-50 t/MW, luminix 42 t/MW — converging within ±50%.
- **SBSP reference designs:** Mankins SPS-ALPHA (modular 50-200 kg / 1-2 GW system), NASA OTPS 2024 cost envelope.
- **Propellant depot demand floor:** Kornuta 2019 / Metzger 2023 450 t/yr near-term baseline.
- **Artemis cargo gap:** NASA M2M 2024+2025 architecture review — 1,500 kg baseline vs multi-tonne need.
- **Mars cadence envelope:** Musk's 1,000-ship-per-window aspirational target as upper bound.
- **Satellite servicing market:** $2.4B (2023) → $5.1B (2030) at 11.5% CAGR.
