============================================================
Sanity checks
============================================================

SBSP TAI-C 100 GW × 5 kg/kW = 500,000 t total over 15 yr
  → 33,000 t/yr SBSP alone. Equivalent to ~165 Starship-200t
    launches/year just for SBSP. Plausibility check:
    Mankins 80,000 t for 4 GW reference design at 20 kg/kW
    → 100 GW at 5 kg/kW = 500,000 t. Consistent.

Depot TAI-C 160,000 t/yr LEO propellant ÷ 100 t/Starship-tanker
  → 1600 tanker flights/year, ~4.4/day.
  Plausibility: SpaceX target 1,000 ships/year production;
  this scenario uses ~1,600 tanker flights/year — broadly
  consistent with the industrial-explosion launch cadence.

SDC TAI-C 1,000 GW × 40 t/MW × 1000 MW/GW = 40,000,000 t
  → 40 Mt over 15 yr = 2.67 Mt/yr. This is at the architectural
    limit of any conceivable Earth launch program. Implies
    cheap orbital deployment requires in-space manufacturing
    (lunar-sourced structural mass) to be tractable.

This last sanity check IS the lunar-manufacturing thesis:
  under TAI-C, orbital demand exceeds plausible Earth-launch
  capacity, which is exactly the condition under which lunar
  manufacturing becomes the binding architecture.
