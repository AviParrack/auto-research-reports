## Abstract

Fortune analysis of the Bezos-Musk space rivalry. Documents the
parallel FCC filings: SpaceX/xAI for "up to 1 million satellites with
AI computing capability"; Blue Origin for ~52,000 AI-capable
satellites. SpaceX stated rationale: "Orbital data centers are the
most efficient way to meet the accelerating demand for AI computing
power." Both companies pursue multi-billion-dollar Artemis HLS
contracts in parallel. Bezos (Turin tech conference): "Space will end
up being one of the places that keeps making earth better." Reuters-
type framing; useful for documenting hyperscaler-adjacent commitments
rather than independent analysis.
