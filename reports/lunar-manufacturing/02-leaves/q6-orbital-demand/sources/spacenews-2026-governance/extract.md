## Abstract

SpaceNews piece on orbital data center governance, framing scaling as
contingent on certification and regulatory infrastructure rather than
purely technical viability. The article references multiple expert
positions including Phil Metzger's industrial-explosion-conditional
viability argument. Metzger: "A lot of the skepticism of data centers
in space probably comes from failing to price in the effects of
exponential expansion. If AI doesn't grow exponentially, then I don't
think it will make sense very soon to put them in space; but I think
it will grow exponentially." This is the central Metzger position on
the SDC market — viability is exponential-AI-growth-conditional.

## Key claims

- metzger-exponential-quote: "If AI doesn't grow exponentially, then
  I don't think it will make sense very soon to put them in space; but
  I think it will grow exponentially."
- governance-gap-as-binding: Certification and regulation, not
  technical viability, currently bottleneck commercial scaling.
- exponential-conditional: Per Metzger, orbital DC viability is
  exponential-AI-growth-conditional.
