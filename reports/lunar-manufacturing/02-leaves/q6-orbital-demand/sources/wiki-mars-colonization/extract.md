## Abstract

Wikipedia overview of SpaceX's Mars colonization program. Reference
data points: program targets 1,000 Starships per Mars launch window
(every 26 months) at full scale; Musk's million-ton target requires
"thousands of Starship launches." Launch cadence (May 2025
presentation): 5 ships in 2026/27 window; ~20 missions in 2028/29
window (75 t each); 100 missions in 2030/31 window (150 t each);
500 missions in 2033 window (300 t each). Cargo-to-Mars capacity per
Starship: 100-150 t reusable initial; up to 300 t per Block 4/expended.
2024 Nature feasibility study (cited) challenges the architecture on
Δv grounds. Tier E orientation; the Musk million-ton claim is
re-attributed to a tier B public-figure statement.
