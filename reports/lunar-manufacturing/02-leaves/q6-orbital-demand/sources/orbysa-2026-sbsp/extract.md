## Abstract

Industry news overview of SBSP industry as of February 2026. Reports
40-50% array efficiency at 10+ km² scale, with 5-10 km² ground
rectennas, targeting 10 GW stations at $0.5-1/W. Key players: Aetherflux
(Galactic Brain orbiting data centers, Q1 2027 launch); China (1 km
solar array by 2028); Caltech + Aetherflux US programs gaining
traction; ESA, Japan, Korea participating. 25-year deorbit rule
compliance noted as regulatory constraint.

## Key claims

- aetherflux-q1-2027: Aetherflux Galactic Brain (orbiting data centers)
  Q1 2027 launch target.
- china-1km-array-2028: China plans 1 km solar array deployment by 2028.
- cost-target-half-dollar-per-watt: 10 GW stations at $0.5-1/W cost
