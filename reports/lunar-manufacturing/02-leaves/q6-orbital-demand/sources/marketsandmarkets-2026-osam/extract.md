## Abstract

Market-research report on the on-orbit satellite servicing sector.
Market projected from $2.4B (2023) to $5.1B (2030), CAGR 11.5%.
Four service categories: active debris removal (ADR) + orbit
adjustment (projected to lead); robotic servicing; refueling
(fluid transfer systems for safe satellite fueling); assembly
(modular satellite construction). Key players: Maxar, Astroscale,
SpaceLogistics LLC, Airbus, Thales Alenia Space. North America
largest share; commercial segment fastest-growing.
