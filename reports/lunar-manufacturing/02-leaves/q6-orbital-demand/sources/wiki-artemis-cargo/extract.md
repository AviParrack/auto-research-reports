## Abstract

Wikipedia overview of the Artemis program. Cadence and payload
references: SLS Block 1 95 t to LEO / 27 t to TLI ($2B/launch); Starship
200 t to LEO / "200 t to TLI estimated" (target $2M/launch);
Blue Origin Blue Moon lander 7 t cargo to TLI ($68M/launch); Foundational
Surface Habitat "commercially built and commercially launched in the
early 2030s." Gateway resupply via ~28 commercial cargo missions over
program life. Artemis V scheduled late 2028; "approximately annual lunar
landings" thereafter. Tier E orientation only; quotable figures
attributed via primary NASA documents in the source-review-eligible
m2m-cargo and m2m-logistics extracts.
