## Abstract

Wikipedia overview of SpaceX Starship payload across blocks: Block 1
15 t to LEO, Block 2 35 t, Block 3 100 t, Block 4 200 t (reusable);
~400 t in expendable mode. Per-Starship-tanker propellant payload
~100 t. Musk-quoted refueling estimate: 8 tanker launches to refill
a single Starship for interplanetary trajectory. NASA-quoted estimate:
16 tanker launches (cryogenic boiloff factored). These ratios are the
key levers for cislunar/Mars propellant depot demand. Tier E
orientation; specific payload figures are widely cross-referenced.
