## Abstract

NVIDIA's 2026 press announcement for the space computing product
portfolio: IGX Thor, Jetson Orin, RTX PRO 6000 Blackwell Server
Edition (all immediately available); Vera Rubin Space-1 Module
(future release). Partner companies named: Aetherflux, Axiom Space,
Kepler Communications, Planet Labs, Sophia Space, Starcloud.
Performance claims: Vera Rubin GPU delivers "up to 25× more AI
compute for space-based inferencing" vs H100; RTX PRO 6000 offers
"up to 100× faster" geospatial analysis vs CPU-based batch.

## Key claims

- partner-companies: Aetherflux, Axiom, Kepler, Planet, Sophia,
  Starcloud confirmed as space-computing partners.
- vera-rubin-performance: 25× more AI compute vs H100 for space-
  based inferencing.
- product-availability: IGX Thor, Jetson Orin, RTX PRO 6000 Blackwell
  available immediately (early 2026); Vera Rubin "to be available at
  a later date."
