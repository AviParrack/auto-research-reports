# SatBase Feb 2026 — Falcon 9 pricing update

Trade-press pricing analysis. Concrete 2026 Falcon 9 numbers + historical trajectory.

## 2026 pricing

- **Dedicated launch:** $74 million (up from $70M, +$4M)
- **Rideshare base:** $350,000 for 50 kg payload
- **Rideshare incremental:** $7,000/kg above base

## Historical rideshare trajectory

| Period | Base rate | $/kg incremental |
|---|---|---|
| Late Feb 2026 | $350,000 (50 kg) | $7,000/kg |
| Aug 2024 | ~$1.2M (200 kg) | $6,000/kg |
| Mar 2023 | ~$1.3M (200 kg) | $6,500/kg |
| Mar 2022 | $1.1M (200 kg) | $5,500/kg |
| 2019–2021 | $1.0M (200 kg) | $5,000/kg |

## Quoted price drivers

> "SpaceX publicly stated that it expected rideshare pricing to increase by approximately $500 per kilogram annually to keep pace with inflation."

> "Since 2024, SpaceX appears to have adopted a structured pricing strategy that increases rideshare rates by roughly $500/kg per year."

Factors cited:
- Global inflation (aluminum, composites, avionics, aerospace labor)
- Capacity constraints — high SSO demand + delays from emerging launch providers
- New FAA payload-weight-based licensing fees beginning 2026 (capped at $30,000 per launch)

## Forward outlook

No explicit 2030+ projection. Article advises: "long-term budget modeling should assume continued incremental increases." Falcon 9 rideshare "remains one of the most cost-effective paths to orbit for payloads under several hundred kilograms."

## Relevance for q1-earth-launch-cost

- Rideshare pricing $7,000/kg is the 2026 consumer-facing rate, not internal cost
- The $500/kg annual structural increase is contra the "Starship will drive costs to $10/kg" thesis — even on Falcon 9 rideshare, *real consumer prices have risen* 2022→2026 by ~$1,500/kg
- The disconnect: internal SpaceX cost (~$629/kg per cross-source) vs published list price ($2,720/kg–$7,000/kg depending on tier). Margin captured by SpaceX, not passed to customers.
- For Starship comparison: any "cost per kg" projection needs to specify *internal cost*, *list price*, or *effective price after rideshare structure*.
